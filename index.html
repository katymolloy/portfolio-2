ABOUT

Hello! My name is Katy and I'm a full-stack junior developer based in Ontario, Canada. Recently graduated with honours from Fanshawe College, I've acquired a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, allowing me to craft robust and user-friendly web applications.

My journey into web development started with an interest in customizing my Tumblr page. Over the years, this interest has transformed into a deep-seated passion. I thrive on challenges and love turning complex problems into simple, elegant solutions.

In my projects, I prioritize clean code, scalability, and user experience. I believe that a well-designed website not only looks good but also functions seamlessly, providing users with an intuitive and enjoyable experience.

I'm always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies, ensuring that I stay updated with the ever-evolving world of web development. If you're interested in working together, please feel free to contact me.
